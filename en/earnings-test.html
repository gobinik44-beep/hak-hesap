Good to know

  • Withheld benefits are not lost forever — at full retirement age your monthly benefit is recalculated to credit the withheld months.
  • Only wages and self-employment earnings count; pensions, investments and withdrawals don't.
  • From the month you reach full retirement age, there is no earnings limit.
